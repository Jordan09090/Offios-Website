Frequently asked questions

Flexible offices provide a modern and convenient workspace solution that offers maximum flexibility. Unlike traditional office leases, flexible offices allow you to rent space on a short-term basis, often month-to-month. This eliminates the need for long-term commitments and reduces upfront costs. Additionally, flexible offices typically operate 24/7, providing you with round-the-clock access to your workspace. With no dedicated staff on-site, you can avoid additional overhead costs, making it a cost-effective solution for businesses of all sizes.

Private offices offer a fully furnished workspace, including a desk, ergonomic chair, and additional seating. Our professional cleaning service ensures a tidy environment, and convenient printing facilities are available on-site.

Of course, we understand that everyone has different comfort preferences. Are your coworkers hotter than you, but you're freezing cold? With our individually controlled air conditioning units, you can customize your workspace to your exact needs. Turn your vent off completely, or adjust the airflow to anywhere between 0 and 100%. No more uncomfortable workdays, only personalized comfort.
